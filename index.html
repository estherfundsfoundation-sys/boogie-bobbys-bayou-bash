ABOUT BOBBY

The man who keeps
the family connected.

Bobby Vincent—lovingly known as Boogie Bobby—was born March 5, 1969, to Willie Vincent and Betty Lou Vincent. His annual birthday celebration gives the Vincent family a reason to gather, laugh, eat well, tell the stories again, and make brand-new ones.

He is husband to Louise Vincent; father to Shayna, Shayla, and Shaquile; brother, uncle, grandfather, and the familiar presence that keeps generations reaching back toward one another.

“Boogie Bobby is the glue that keeps the family connected.”
